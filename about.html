About Us

Inspiring Hope,
Innovating Health

Captain Herbal Hospital is renowned for being one of Ghana's premier herbal hospital established on August 15, 2015, adhering strictly to the standards set by the Ministry of Health. Specializing in herbal medications and nutritional food supplements, we offer high-quality, comprehensive wellness services to the people of Ghana and beyond.

Our commitment to excellence drives us to deliver high-quality, comprehensive wellness services to the people of Ghana and the broader global community. Before our formal establishment, our esteemed founder, Dr. Emmanuel Amokwandoh (Captain), dedicated himself to the cause by reaching out to communities, moving door-to-door to promote and distribute his herbal solutions. This unwavering dedication has solidified Captain Herbal Hospital as leading names in the nation's herbal healthcare sector.
